Scrobbling

To use this, you'll need to be scrobbling. Your music application sends data to the Last.fm scrobbling service, which is published on the web. Seeing-is-Scrobbling reads from Last.fm's scrobbling data.

Nearly every internet-connected music application already has scrobbling attached, you just need to enable it and point it at your Last.fm account.

Hint: In Mac Spotify, you can do this by going to Spotify->Preferences, and select 'Scrobble to Last.fm'.
